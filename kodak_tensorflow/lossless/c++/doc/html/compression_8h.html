Compresses signed integers without loss.

The lossless coder combines a binarizer and a binary arithmetic coder.

Parameters
sizeSize of array_input and array_output.
array_inputSigned integers to be compressed without loss.
array_outputReconstruction of the signed integers after the compression without loss.
truncated_unary_lengthLength of the truncated unary prefix.
probabilitiesIts ith element is the probability that the ith binary decision is 0 in the truncated unary prefix.

Coding cost of the signed integers. The coding cost is expressed in bits.
